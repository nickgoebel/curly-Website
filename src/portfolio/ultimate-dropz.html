2024 - 2025

Ultimate Dropz

From 2024 to 2025 I collaborated with Ultimate Dropz, one of the most exclusive trading card companies in the world. My work included leading the design for a full team box celebrating Bayer 04 Leverkusen’s historic championship season and creating visuals for the German Basketball National Team at the Olympics. In addition, I supported the agency with trade fair design, ensuring that the premium and storytelling-driven identity of Ultimate Dropz was reflected across all projects.
