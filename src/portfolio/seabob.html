2022 - 2025

SEABOB

From 2022 to 2025 I worked with SEABOB on a wide range of exciting projects. Highlights included collaborations with Studio F. A. Porsche and Automobili Lamborghini, where I developed visuals and special editions. Beyond these projects I was responsible for designing catalogs, trade fair stands, campaign visuals, social media content, and press kits. My role also included photo retouching as well as supporting the development of a website and a mobile app, ensuring a consistent and premium visual identity across all channels.
