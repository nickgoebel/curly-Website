2025

Robert Andrich

In 2025 I created an artwork for Robert Andrich that illustrates his path from the Regionalliga through the 2. Bundesliga and into the Bundesliga, where he achieved three major titles with Bayer Leverkusen. The piece also highlights his debut for the German national team and his iconic hairstyle at EURO 2024, while capturing the fact that he now competes in the Champions League. This artwork honors his perseverance and the milestones of his career.
