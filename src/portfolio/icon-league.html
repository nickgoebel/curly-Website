2024 - 2025

The Icon League

Between 2024 and 2025 I created multiple visuals for the Icon League by Toni Kroos and Elias Nerlich. Among them was a three-slide design for their Instagram account showcasing all team heads and the arenas of Season 1 during the very first Draft Day. Designing for this project is always a pleasure as I enjoy creative freedom, and it is especially meaningful to me since I follow the Icon League myself on Twitch every week.
